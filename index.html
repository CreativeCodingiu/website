About Us

Creative Coding Club has open studio sessions where anyone can work on their projects and collaborate. Along with studio sessions, Creative Coding Club has workshops to show club members what sort of projects are possible and to give foundational tech or art skills. All in all, Creative Coding Club wants to be a safe and welcoming space where people can make awesome artwork through code and other digital or physical technologies.
